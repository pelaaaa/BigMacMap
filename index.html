Big Mac Cop in Oregon

Big Mac Cop gives you a tour of McDonald's through the state of Oregon. There are 158 McDonald's locations within the state,
and Big Mac Cop knows all of them. Tell him where to go by clicking on the map. He can then tell you where the Mcdonald's locations
are within a certain radius that you set as well as the price of Big Macs at that location. He is very knowledgable. If you talk to
him he can give you a lot of information on the area.

Big Mac Cop likes to use this method as a way of showing the Big Mac index. Created by the Economist magazine, the Big Mac index
is a tongue-in-cheek way of showing different price levels between countries. Knowledge of price level influence exchange rates and any
disparities between them. While economists do not actually use the Big mac index to decide exchange rates, it is fun way of generalizing
possible realities. This Wikipedia article talks about it more.
